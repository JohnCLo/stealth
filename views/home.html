off-the-record conversations on slack



How It Works


/stealth

Type /stealth in any Slack room and click on the link that appears. A private room will be generated where you can chat off-the-record.


What is Stealth?




Leaves No Trace

Once messages are read they disappear forever. Nothing is stored.

Private

We automatically limit the chat room to only those who are supposed be there.

Encrypted

All messages are encrypted using 256-bit AES.



Supports Groups

Chat rooms can support up to 20 people.

Seamless

Go from Slack to Stealth and back with just one click.

Platform Agnostic

Works on any platform, any desktop or mobile device.
